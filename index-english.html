Undoubtedly the UX/UI is the most expressive part of my experience.

I started my career working with creation, in the beginning the idea was just to make everything beautiful, but little by little I was looking more and more to understand my user in different ways. At a certain point, I understood that I needed something new. I fell in love with the study of user behavior and when I finally understood this side of me, I took on a new career and directed my studies to UX and UI.

As a UX/UI designer I like to look for immersive solutions. How about we play a little while we test our knowledge?
